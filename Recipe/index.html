how to make fluffy persian rice

by moj


cook time: 24 min
servings: 2-3


ingredients




  • two quarts of water
  • two tablespoons kosher salt
  • two tablespoons olive oil
  • two cups basmati rice
  • one potato, cut into thin slices
  • a few slices of butter
  • a pinch of saffron threads
  • a pinch of chopped parsley


directions



step one
boil 2 cups of rice in a pot with the kosher salt for exatcly 7 minutes and then drain the water

step two
heat up the olive oil in a pot and then cover the bottom of the pot with slices of potato. sprinkle some salt and let them sizzle for 2 minutes. you may now cover the potatos with rice and add a few slices of butter on the top. reduce heat to low.

step three
top the pot with a layer of clean paper towel then place the lid over it. steam until rice is fluffy. this should take approximately 17-20 minutes

step four
grind saffron threads and mix it with 1 1/2 table spoon of hot water in a bowl. add a couple of spoonfuls of rice to saffron mixture and stir until the rice is yellow.

step five
in a serving dish, add the rice and top it with saffron rice. add the now crunchy potatoes on the side. garnish with parsley and BON APPETIT
